# DebugMaster

A professional debugging platform designed for developer drills. Master the art of technical restraint.

## The Seniority Trap

DebugMaster isn't just about passing tests. It's about how you pass them. We reward **Senior Behavior**:
- **Restraint**: Fewer `console.log` calls mean higher scores.
- **Precision**: Faster fixes with minimal code changes.
- **Senior Mode**: A high-stakes toggle that penalizes "noise" and evaluates your technical judgment.

## Project Philosophy (Taste & Judgment)

### Taste: Why this?
We chose to build a "Debug First" arena rather than a generic LeetCode clone. Most platforms focus on *writing* code, but seniors spend 80% of their time *fixing* it. We left out complex backends and global rankings to focus on the immediate, visceral feedback loop of squashing a bug.

### Judgment: The Smallest Interesting Version
We focused on three core scenarios: stale state, infinite loops, and memory leaks. These are the most common "silent killers" in React apps. We built a browser-only runner to ensure instant setup and zero friction—maximizing "Shipping Ability."

### Originality: The Seniority Report
Upon solving a challenge, you don't just get a checkmark. You get a **Seniority Analysis**—a post-fix review that compares your behavior against senior standards, offering a "Mental Model" for why the bug happened in the first place.
